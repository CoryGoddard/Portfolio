The Pythagorean Theorem

Formula: a² + b² = c²

Description: This formula is used to calculate the length of a hypotenuse, or the side of a right triangle opposite of the right angle. It states that the sum of the squares of side A and side B, the sides creating the right angle, are equal to the square of side C, or the hypotenuse.
